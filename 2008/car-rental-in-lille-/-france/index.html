Car Rental in Lille / France

For those who would like to not only visit Lille, but would like to tour the “Nord de France” – some post on our recommended visits incoming – please check with the following 3 car rental companies:

Europcar
Avis
Hertz

The reason for this selection are quite simple. They are the biggest, largest, cheapest and most convenient car rental companies I know of. The main advantage to you is that you can pick up a car at one place and return it at a different one – making your life a lot easier !

For those coming from Malaysia, the road in France are very much similar to the Malaysian ones. Only tiny minuscule difference is that we drive on the other side of the road… But all signaletic and colors are the same – Malaysians Highways were built by French contractors.

You can also rent a GPS for something like 10 Euro per day. Making your life a lot easier !!
